Patriot Roll of Honor

Updated: FY2026 Q2  |  Source: CLEAR Civic Engagement Tracking System  |  Scores as of: 2026-04-01

The Bureau recognizes citizens who have demonstrated exceptional civic commitment through consistent use of the CLEAR application and sustained high Patriot Scores. These individuals serve as a model for all citizens. Their identities are protected per citizen privacy regulations; rankings are displayed by CLEAR citizen identifier.

Your Score: To view your own Patriot Score and standing, open the CLEAR application. Citizens with scores above 130 are automatically eligible for Roll of Honor recognition.

How to Improve Your Score

Citizens who wish to improve their Patriot Score are encouraged to install and actively use the CLEAR application. CLEAR tracks daily civic obligations and awards Patriot Points for timely completion. Common score-improving activities include: property maintenance, appointment adherence, financial obligation fulfillment, and routine household upkeep.

Citizens are advised that repeated deferral of obligations via the snooze function will result in score penalties. The Bureau notes that a pattern of avoidance is statistically correlated with ideological non-compliance.
