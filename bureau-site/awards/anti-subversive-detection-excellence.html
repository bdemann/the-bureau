About This Award

The Anti-Subversive Detection Excellence Award is presented annually by the Office of Ideological Compliance to the Bureau agent or officer who has demonstrated the most outstanding record of identifying, documenting, and referring ideologically suspect individuals for compliance review during the fiscal year.

The award was established in FY2019 following a significant uptick in detected subversive activity across the Bureau's monitored districts. The recipient is determined entirely by confirmed identification volume, as verified by the Compliance Review Board. Character references and seniority are not considered.

Note: The Bureau's selection process is administered independently by the Office of Ideological Compliance and is not subject to Directorate override. All nominations are reviewed by a panel of three senior compliance officers, none of whom report directly to the Bureau Director.
