About This Award

The Field Agent of the Quarter commendation is issued each quarter by the Bureau Directorate to one field agent whose conduct, case outcomes, and civic impact have distinguished them above their peers. Selection is based on citizen-reported satisfaction scores, case closure rates, and the Directorate's internal performance metrics.

The recipient is named on this page for the duration of the quarter. This page will be updated following each quarterly review cycle. Previous recipients are archived in the Bureau's internal records and are not publicly listed.
